You found us! Great to see you, friend.

All around us, the seasons are changing. The Earth is changing daily, seasonally, climatically. At Rogue Pickings, we tune in to the changes and bring them to you daily. We partner with local farms to showcase the bounty of the seasons for your enjoyment. And we use biofuel in our truck because anything we can do to lessen our heavy burden on the Earth is a win. From farm to our food truck to your mouth, we aim to please!
